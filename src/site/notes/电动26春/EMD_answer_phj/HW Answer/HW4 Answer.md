w&= \frac{1}{2}\left( \epsilon_0E^2 + \frac{B^2}{\mu_0} \right) \geq \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon_0}{\mu_0}} EB, \quad{д»…еҪ“\epsilon_0E^2 + \frac{B^2}{\mu_0}ж—¶еҸ–зӯүеҸ·}

cg&= \frac{P}{c} = \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon_0}{\mu_0}} |\boldsymbol{E} \times \boldsymbol{B} | \leq  \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon_0}{\mu_0}} EB, \quad{д»…еҪ“\boldsymbol{E} \perp \boldsymbol{B}ж—¶еҸ–зӯүеҸ·}

## 1.2 
д»ҘдёӯзӮ№дёәеҺҹзӮ№пјҢиҝһзәҝдёәzиҪҙе»әз«Ӣжҹұеқҗж Үзі»пјҢз”өиҚ·еқҗж Ү $(0,0,\pm a)$пјҢеҲҷдёӯеһӮйқўдёҠд»»ж„ҸдёҖзӮ№зҡ„з”өеңәжІҝзқҖжҹұеқҗж Ү $\hat{s}$ ж–№еҗ‘

E_s = 2 \cdot \frac{q}{4\pi\epsilon_0 {s^2+a^2}} \cdot \frac{s}{\sqrt{s^2+a^2}} = \frac{q}{2\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{s}{ ({s^2+a^2})^{3/2}}

$$
жүҖд»Ҙ  
$$
\begin{align} 
F_z  &= \int_0^{+\infty}  \frac{q^2}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{s^3}{ ({s^2+a^2})^{3}} \,\mathrm{d}s \\[4pt]
{\color{gray} u = s^2 +a^2 \to}

$$

# 2 жҖқиҖғйўҳ  
## 2.1 зЈҒеҒ¶жһҒеӯҗ/еёҰз”өж—ӢиҪ¬зҗғ
### 2.1.1 
зҗғеЈідёҠзҡ„йқўз”өжөҒеҜҶеәҰ $\boldsymbol{K}(\boldsymbol{x}')$ жҳҜз”ұиЎЁйқўз”өиҚ·йҡҸзҗғеЈіиҪ¬еҠЁдә§з”ҹзҡ„пјҢе…¶иЎЁиҫҫејҸдёәпјҡ
$$
\boldsymbol{K}(\boldsymbol{x}') = \sigma_0 \boldsymbol{v} = \sigma_0 (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}')
$$
е…¶дёӯ $\boldsymbol{x}'$ дёәзҗғеЈідёҠзҡ„жәҗзӮ№дҪҚзҪ®зҹўйҮҸпјҢдё” $|\boldsymbol{x}'| = a$гҖӮжүҖд»ҘзЈҒзҹўеҠҝдёә
$$
\begin{align} 
\boldsymbol{A}(\boldsymbol{x}) 
&= \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \oint_S \frac{\sigma_0 (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}')}{|\boldsymbol{x} - \boldsymbol{x}'|} \,\mathrm{d}\sigma' 
\\[4pt]
&=\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0}{4\pi} \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \oint_S \frac{\boldsymbol{x}'}{|\boldsymbol{x} - \boldsymbol{x}'|} \,\mathrm{d}\sigma'
\\[4pt]
\end{align}
$$
д»Өз§ҜеҲҶйғЁеҲҶдёә $\boldsymbol{I} = \oint_S \frac{\boldsymbol{x}'}{|\boldsymbol{x} - \boldsymbol{x}'|} \,\mathrm{d}\sigma'$гҖӮи®ҫ $\boldsymbol{x}' = a \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}'$пјҢйқўе…ғ $\,\mathrm{d}\sigma' = a^2 \,\mathrm{d}\Omega'$гҖӮзҗғеқҗж Үзі»дёӢпјҲеҸӮи§Ғ [[A_symmetry_иҮӘз„¶з•Ңзҡ„еҜ№з§°жҖ§/жқӮи®°/3-D Laplace Equation And Spherical Harmonic Function#4.1 еҮҪж•° $ frac{1}{ vec{r}- vec{a} }$ зҡ„еӢ’и®©еҫ·зі»ж•°\|3-D Laplace Equation And Spherical Harmonic Function#4.1 еҮҪж•° $ frac{1}{ vec{r}- vec{a} }$ зҡ„еӢ’и®©еҫ·зі»ж•°]]пјүпјҡ
$$
\frac{1}{|\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{x'}|} = \sum_{l=0}^\infty  \frac{r_<^l}{r^{l+1}_>} P_l(\cos\gamma), \quad \gamma=\arccos\frac{\boldsymbol{x}\cdot\boldsymbol{x'}}{|\boldsymbol{x}\cdot\boldsymbol{x'}|},\  \quad r_< = \min(x,x'), \ r_> = \max(x,x')
$$
еҲҷ
$$\boldsymbol{I} = a^3 \sum_{l=0}^{\infty} \frac{r_<^l}{r_>^{l+1}} \oint_S P_l(\cos\gamma) \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}' \,\mathrm{d}\Omega'$$
з”ұдәҺ $\hat{\boldsymbol{r}}'$ еҸҜд»ҘзңӢдҪңжҳҜз”ұ $l=1$ зҡ„зҗғи°җеҮҪж•°зәҝжҖ§з»„еҗҲиҖҢжҲҗпјҢж №жҚ®жӯЈдәӨжҖ§пјҢеҸӘжңү $l=1$ зҡ„йЎ№еҜ№еә”зҡ„з§ҜеҲҶдёҚдёәйӣ¶гҖӮеҪ“ $l=1$ ж—¶пјҢ$P_1(\cos\gamma) = \cos\gamma = \hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}'$пјҢж•…пјҡ
$$\boldsymbol{I} = a^3 \frac{r_<}{r_>^2} \oint_S (\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}') \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}' \,\mathrm{d}\Omega'$$
еҲ©з”ЁиҜҫдёҠзҡ„ $\oint_S \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}' \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}' \,\mathrm{d}\Omega' = \frac{4\pi}{3} \mathsf{I}$пјҢеҫ—еҲ°пјҡ
$$\boldsymbol{I} = a^3 \frac{r_<}{r_>^2} \frac{4\pi}{3} \hat{\boldsymbol{r}} = \frac{4\pi a^3}{3} \frac{r_<}{r_>^2} \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}$$
жүҖд»Ҙ
$$
\boldsymbol{A}(\boldsymbol{x}) = \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0}{4\pi} \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \left( \frac{4\pi a^3}{3} \frac{r_<}{r_>^2} \hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \right) = \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^3}{3} \frac{r_<}{r_>^2} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})
$$
$$
\boldsymbol{A}_{in}(\boldsymbol{x}) = \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^3}{3} \frac{r}{a^2} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \frac{\boldsymbol{x}}{r}) = {\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a}{3} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x})}, \quad r< a
$$
$$
\boldsymbol{A}_{out}(\boldsymbol{x}) = \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^3}{3} \frac{a}{r^2} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \frac{\boldsymbol{x}}{r}) = {\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^4}{3 r^3} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x})}, \quad r>a
$$
### 2.1.2 
зЈҒж„ҹеә”ејәеәҰз”ұ $\boldsymbol{B} = \nabla \times \boldsymbol{A}$ з»ҷеҮәгҖӮеҲ©з”Ё $\nabla \times (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}) = \boldsymbol{\omega}(\nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{x}) - (\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \nabla)\boldsymbol{x} = 3\boldsymbol{\omega} - \boldsymbol{\omega} = 2\boldsymbol{\omega}$пјҢеҸҜеҫ—пјҡ
$$
\boldsymbol{B}_{in} = \nabla \times \left[ \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a}{3} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}) \right] = {\frac{2}{3} \mu_0 \sigma_0 a \boldsymbol{\omega}}  
$$
еҜ№дәҺзҗғеӨ–еңәпјҢеҲ©з”Ё $\nabla \times (f\boldsymbol{V}) = \nabla f \times \boldsymbol{V} + f(\nabla \times \boldsymbol{V})$пјҢи®ҫ $f(r) = r^{-3}$пјҢ$\boldsymbol{V} = \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}$пјҡ
$$\nabla(r^{-3}) \times (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}) = -3r^{-4}\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \times (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}) = -3r^{-5} \boldsymbol{x} \times (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x})$$
еұ•ејҖдёүйҮҚеҸүз§Ҝ $\boldsymbol{x} \times (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}) = \boldsymbol{\omega}(\boldsymbol{x} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}) - \boldsymbol{x}(\boldsymbol{x} \cdot \boldsymbol{\omega}) = r^2\boldsymbol{\omega} - r^2(\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})\hat{\boldsymbol{r}}$пјҢжүҖд»Ҙ
$$\nabla \times \left( \frac{\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x}}{r^3} \right) = -3r^{-5}[r^2\boldsymbol{\omega} - r^2(\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})\hat{\boldsymbol{r}}] + r^{-3}(2\boldsymbol{\omega}) = \frac{1}{r^3} [3(\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} - \boldsymbol{\omega}]$$
д»Је…Ҙзі»ж•°пјҢеҫ—еҲ°зҗғеӨ–зҡ„зЈҒеңәеҲҶеёғпјҡ
$$\boldsymbol{B}_{out} = {\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^4}{3 r^3} [3(\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} - \boldsymbol{\omega}]}$$
жҳҫз„¶иҝҷжҳҜдёҖдёӘзЈҒеҒ¶жһҒеӯҗеңәпјҢе…¶зЈҒзҹ©дёә $\boldsymbol{m} = \frac{4\pi}{3}\sigma_0 \boldsymbol{\omega} a^4$гҖӮ
### 2.1.3 
е’ҢиҜҫдёҠеёҰз”өзҗғеҸ—еҠӣзҡ„жұӮи§Јзұ»дјјпјҢжҲ‘д»¬йҖүжӢ©еҢ…еӣҙж•ҙдёӘдёҠеҚҠз©әй—ҙпјҲ$z > 0$пјүзҡ„й—ӯеҗҲжӣІйқў $S$гҖӮиҝҷдёӘй—ӯеҗҲжӣІйқўеҢ…жӢ¬ж— з©·иҝңеӨ„зҡ„еҚҠзҗғйқўпјҲ$r \to \infty, z > 0$пјүе’ҢиөӨйҒ“е№ійқўпјҲ$z = 0$пјүгҖӮ

з”ұдәҺеңЁж— з©·иҝңеӨ„пјҢеҒ¶жһҒеӯҗзЈҒеңә $B \propto 1/r^3$пјҢжүҖд»Ҙеј йҮҸеҲҶйҮҸ $T \propto 1/r^6$гҖӮиҖҢж— з©·иҝңеҚҠзҗғйқўзҡ„йқўз§Ҝе…ғ $\mathrm{d}a \propto r^2$пјҢеӣ жӯӨеҪ“ $r \to \infty$ ж—¶пјҢж— з©·иҝңеҚҠзҗғйқўдёҠзҡ„з§ҜеҲҶйЎ№и¶Ӣиҝ‘дәҺ $0$гҖӮ

жүҖд»ҘпјҢжҲ‘д»¬еҸӘйңҖиҰҒи®Ўз®—еңЁиөӨйҒ“е№ійқўпјҲ$z=0$пјүдёҠзҡ„з§ҜеҲҶгҖӮеҜ№дәҺдёҠеҚҠз©әй—ҙпјҢйқўе…ғеҗ‘йҮҸдёә $\mathrm{d}\boldsymbol{a} = -\hat{\boldsymbol{z}} \,\mathrm{d}a$гҖӮ

з”ұдәҺеҜ№з§°жҖ§пјҢиөӨйҒ“йқўдёҠзҡ„зЈҒеҠӣеҸӘеҸҜиғҪжңү $z$ еҲҶйҮҸпјҲеҫ„еҗ‘еҸ—еҠӣзӣёдә’жҠөж¶Ҳпјүпјҡ
$$F_z = \int_{z=0} (\mathsf{T} \cdot \mathrm{d}\boldsymbol{a})_z = \int_{z=0} T_{zz} (-\mathrm{d}a) = -\int_{z=0} T_{zz} \,\mathrm{d}a$$
йәҰе…Ӣж–ҜйҹҰеә”еҠӣеј йҮҸзҡ„зәҜзЈҒеңәйғЁеҲҶе®ҡд№үдёә $T_{ij} = \frac{1}{\mu_0} \left( B_i B_j - \frac{1}{2}\delta_{ij}B^2 \right)$пјҡ
$$T_{zz}^{in} = \frac{1}{2\mu_0} \left( \frac{2}{3} \mu_0 \sigma_0 a \omega \right)^2 = \frac{2}{9} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^2 \omega^2$$
жүҖд»Ҙ
$$F_{z}^{in} = -\int_0^a \left( \frac{2}{9} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^2 \omega^2 \right) 2\pi r \,\mathrm{d}r = -\frac{4\pi}{9} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^2 \omega^2 \left[ \frac{1}{2}r^2 \right]_0^a = {-\frac{2\pi}{9} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^4 \omega^2}$$
еңЁиөӨйҒ“йқўдёҠпјҲ$\boldsymbol{\omega} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}} = 0$пјүпјҢеӨ–йғЁзЈҒеңәйҖҖеҢ–дёә $\boldsymbol{B}_{out} = -\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^4 \omega}{3r^3} \hat{\boldsymbol{z}}$пјҡ
$$T_{zz}^{out} = \frac{1}{2\mu_0} \left( -\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a^4 \omega}{3r^3} \right)^2 = \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^8 \omega^2}{18 r^6}$$
жүҖд»Ҙ
$$F_{z}^{out} = -\int_a^\infty \left( \frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^8 \omega^2}{18 r^6} \right) 2\pi r \,\mathrm{d}r = -\frac{\pi}{9} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^8 \omega^2 \int_a^\infty r^{-5} \,\mathrm{d}r =-\frac{\pi}{36} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^4 \omega^2$$
жңҖз»Ҳеҫ—еҲ°
$$F_z = F_{z}^{in} + F_{z}^{out} = {-\frac{\pi}{4} \mu_0 \sigma_0^2 a^4 \omega^2}$$
иҙҹеҸ·д»ЈиЎЁж–№еҗ‘жңқдёӢпјҲеҚ—еҚҠзҗғж–№еҗ‘пјүпјҢеҚідёӨеҚҠзҗғд№Ӣй—ҙзӣёдә’еҗёеј•гҖӮ
## 2.2 з”өеӯҗз»Ҹе…ёзҗғеЈіжЁЎеһӢ
### 2.2.1 
еҜ№дәҺеҚҠеҫ„дёә $a$гҖҒз”өйҮҸдёә $e$ зҡ„еқҮеҢҖеёҰз”өзҗғеЈіпјҢзҗғеҶ…з”өеңәдёә $0$пјҢзҗғеӨ–з”өеңәдёә $E = \frac{e}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r^2}$пјҢ$\sigma_0=\frac{e}{4\pi a^2}$пјҢ
$$W_E = \int_{a}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_0 E^2 (4\pi r^2) \mathrm{d}r = \frac{e^2}{8\pi\epsilon_0 a}$$
еҲ©з”ЁдёҠдёҖйўҳз»“и®әпјҢеҶ…йғЁзЈҒеңә $B_{in} = \frac{\mu_0 e \omega}{6\pi a}$пјҢеӨ–йғЁдёәеҒ¶жһҒеңәгҖӮ

> [!info] $W_M = \frac{1}{2} \oint \boldsymbol{A} \cdot \boldsymbol{K} \mathrm{d}a$
> $$\int_{all} \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0} \mathrm{d}V = \frac{1}{2\mu_0} \int \boldsymbol{B} \cdot (\nabla \times \boldsymbol{A}) \mathrm{d}V$$
> еҲ©з”ЁеҲҶйғЁз§ҜеҲҶпјҡ
> $$\int \boldsymbol{B} \cdot (\nabla \times \boldsymbol{A}) \mathrm{d}V = \int \boldsymbol{A} \cdot (\nabla \times \boldsymbol{B}) \mathrm{d}V + \oint_{\infty} (\boldsymbol{A} \times \boldsymbol{B}) \cdot \mathrm{d}\boldsymbol{S}$$
> еҜ№дәҺеұҖеҹҹеҲҶеёғзҡ„жәҗпјҢж— з©·иҝңеӨ„зҡ„иЎЁйқўз§ҜеҲҶдёә 0гҖӮд»Је…Ҙе®үеҹ№зҺҜи·Ҝе®ҡзҗҶ $\nabla \times \boldsymbol{B} = \mu_0 \boldsymbol{J}$пјҡ
> $$\int_{all} \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0} \mathrm{d}V = \frac{1}{2\mu_0} \int \boldsymbol{A} \cdot (\mu_0 \boldsymbol{J}) \mathrm{d}V = \frac{1}{2} \int \boldsymbol{A} \cdot \boldsymbol{J} \mathrm{d}V$$

е·ІзҹҘзҗғйқўдёҠзҡ„зҹўйҮҸеҠҝдёә $\boldsymbol{A}(a) ={\frac{\mu_0 \sigma_0 a}{3} (\boldsymbol{\omega} \times \boldsymbol{x})}= \frac{\mu_0 e \omega a \sin\theta}{12\pi} \hat{\boldsymbol{\phi}}$пјҢйқўз”өжөҒеҜҶеәҰдёә $\boldsymbol{K} =\sigma_0 \boldsymbol{v} = \sigma_0 a\sin\theta \omega= \frac{e \omega \sin\theta}{4\pi a} \hat{\boldsymbol{\phi}}$гҖӮжүҖд»ҘзЈҒиғҪпјҡ    
$$W_M = \frac{1}{2} \int_0^\pi \left( \frac{\mu_0 e \omega a \sin\theta}{12\pi} \right) \left( \frac{e \omega \sin\theta}{4\pi a} \right) (2\pi a^2 \sin\theta) \mathrm{d}\theta = \frac{\mu_0 e^2 \omega^2 a}{36\pi}$$  
жҖ»иғҪйҮҸ
$$W = \frac{e^2}{8\pi\epsilon_0 a} + \frac{\mu_0 e^2 \omega^2 a}{36\pi}$$

### 2.2.2 
зЈҒзҹ© $\boldsymbol{m} = \frac{4\pi}{3}\sigma_0 \boldsymbol{\omega} a^4=\frac{1}{3}e\boldsymbol{\omega}a^2$пјҢз”өзЈҒеңәи§’еҠЁйҮҸ  
$$
\boldsymbol{L} = \int \boldsymbol{r} \times \boldsymbol{g} \,\mathrm{d}V =  \int \boldsymbol{r} \times (\epsilon_0 \boldsymbol{E} \times \boldsymbol{B}) \,\mathrm{d}V
$$
з”ұдәҺзҗғеҶ… $E=0$пјҢи§’еҠЁйҮҸеҸӘеӯҳеңЁдәҺзҗғеӨ–пјҢи®Ўз®—еҠЁйҮҸйғЁеҲҶпјҡ  
$$\boldsymbol{E} \times \boldsymbol{B} = \left( \frac{e}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \right) \times \left( \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi r^3} [- \boldsymbol{m}] \right) = \frac{\mu_0 e}{16\pi^2 \epsilon_0 r^5} (\boldsymbol{m} \times \hat{\boldsymbol{r}})$$
жүҖд»ҘпјҢиў«з§ҜеҮҪж•°зҡ„зҹўйҮҸйғЁеҲҶжҳҜпјҡ 
$$\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \times (\boldsymbol{m} \times \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}) = \boldsymbol{m}(\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}) - \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}(\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \boldsymbol{m}) = \boldsymbol{m} - \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}(\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \boldsymbol{m})$$
д»Је…ҘеҲ°жұӮи§Ји§’еҠЁйҮҸзҡ„з§ҜеҲҶеҫ—еҲ°
$$
\boldsymbol{L} = \frac{\mu_0 e}{16\pi^2} \int_{a}^{\infty} \frac{1}{r^4} r^2\, \mathrm{d}r \int_{\Omega} [\boldsymbol{m} - \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}(\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \boldsymbol{m})]\, \mathrm{d}\Omega
$$
жӢҶи§ЈиҜҘз§ҜеҲҶеҪ“дёӯзҡ„жҜҸдёҖйЎ№жңүпјҡ  
$$
\int_{a}^{\infty} r^{-2} \, \mathrm{d}r = \frac{1}{a}
$$
$$
\int \boldsymbol{m} \,\mathrm{d}\Omega = 4\pi \boldsymbol{m}
$$
$$
\int \hat{\boldsymbol{r}}(\hat{\boldsymbol{r}} \cdot \boldsymbol{m})\,\mathrm{d}\Omega =\int (\mathsf{I} \cdot \boldsymbol{m}) \,\mathrm{d}\Omega
= \frac{4\pi}{3} \boldsymbol{m}
$$
жүҖд»ҘжңҖз»Ҳеҫ—еҲ°ж—ӢиҪ¬зҗғеЈізҡ„еңәи§’еҠЁйҮҸдёәпјҡ
$$
\boldsymbol{L} = \frac{\mu_0 e}{16\pi^2} \cdot \frac{1}{a} \cdot \frac{8\pi}{3} \boldsymbol{m} = \frac{\mu_0 e}{6\pi a} \boldsymbol{m}
={\frac{\mu_0 e^2 a}{18\pi} \boldsymbol{\omega}}
$$


### 2.2.3 
йҖҡеёёеңЁз»Ҹе…ёжЁЎеһӢдёӯпјҢз”ұдәҺ $\omega a \ll c$ зҡ„йў„жңҹпјҢзЈҒиғҪйЎ№ $W_M$ иҝңе°ҸдәҺйқҷз”өиғҪ $W_E$гҖӮжҲ‘д»¬е…ҲеҝҪз•Ҙ $W_M$пјҡ

з”ұ $W \approx \frac{e^2}{8\pi\epsilon_0 a} = m_e c^2$ еҫ— $a = \frac{e^2}{8\pi\epsilon_0 m_e c^2}$ (еҚіз»Ҹе…ёз”өеӯҗеҚҠеҫ„)пјӣз”ұи§’еҠЁйҮҸе…¬ејҸ $\frac{\mu_0 e^2 a \omega}{18\pi} = \frac{\hbar}{2}$ еҫ— $\omega = \frac{9\pi \hbar}{\mu_0 e^2 a}$гҖӮд»ҘеҸҠ
$$v = \omega a = \frac{9\pi \hbar}{\mu_0 e^2} = \frac{9\pi \hbar \epsilon_0 c^2}{e^2}$$
еҲ©з”ЁзІҫз»Ҷз»“жһ„еёёж•° $\alpha = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\epsilon_0 \hbar c} \approx \frac{1}{137}$пјҢдёҠејҸеҸҜж”№еҶҷдёәпјҡ
$$v = \frac{9}{4 \alpha} c \approx \frac{9 \times 137}{4} c \approx {308 c}$$
иҝҷдёӘйҖҹеәҰиҝңиҝңи¶…и¶ҠдәҶе…үйҖҹпјҢжҳҜжІЎжңүж„Ҹд№үзҡ„пјҢеӣһиҝҮеӨҙжқҘеҶҚз”Ё $W\approx W_M =  \frac{\mu_0 e^2 \omega^2 a}{36\pi} = m_e c^2$ д№ҹжІЎд»Җд№Ҳж„Ҹд№үгҖӮеҸҰеӨ–пјҢжҚ®иҜҙе°Ҷз”өеӯҗи§ҶдёәеҲҡдҪ“пјҢйҖҡиҝҮиҪ¬еҠЁжғҜйҮҸжқҘи®Ўз®—и§’еҠЁйҮҸпјҢд№ҹдјҡеҫ—еҲ°зұ»дјјзҡ„з»“и®әпјҲеҫ—еҲ°иөӨйҒ“и§’йҖҹеәҰдёәдёҖзҷҫеӨҡеҖҚе…үйҖҹпјүгҖӮ
